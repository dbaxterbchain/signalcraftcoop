# Brand System

This is the initial brand system for Signalcraft Coop. It is intentionally minimal and can evolve as the product grows.

## Brand keywords
- Precision
- Signal
- Craft
- Trust
- Modern

## Visual direction
- White + blue foundation with a crisp, futuristic tone.
- Subtle glow and glass accents, not heavy neon.
- Technical but friendly, not sterile.

## Color palette

Core
- `#0A2A43`  Deep Navy (primary text, headings)
- `#1677C8`  Signal Blue (primary action)
- `#52B9FF`  Electric Blue (accent / focus)
- `#EAF4FF`  Ice Blue (surface tint)
- `#FFFFFF`  White (base)

Support
- `#0E1116`  Graphite (dark text on light surfaces)
- `#6B7B8C`  Slate (secondary text)
- `#C5D6E5`  Mist (borders, dividers)
- `#F4F8FB`  Cloud (subtle background)

Status
- Success: `#12B981`
- Warning: `#F59E0B`
- Error: `#EF4444`
- Info: `#3B82F6`

Gradients
- `signalGlow`: radial-gradient(120% 120% at 0% 0%, #52B9FF 0%, #EAF4FF 35%, #FFFFFF 70%)
- `deepField`: linear-gradient(135deg, #0A2A43 0%, #143B59 50%, #1C4C6E 100%)

## Typography

Primary family
- Display: "Space Grotesk" (700, 600)
- Body: "Inter" (400, 500)

Fallbacks
- "Space Grotesk", "Inter", "Segoe UI", Arial, sans-serif

Sizing
- Display: 48 / 56
- H1: 36 / 44
- H2: 28 / 36
- H3: 22 / 30
- Body: 16 / 24
- Small: 14 / 20

## Logo usage (placeholder)
- Primary: wordmark left, icon to the left.
- Clear space: 0.5x logo height.
- Minimum size: 120px width.

## Imagery
- Macro shots of materials (embroidery, laser etch, texture).
- Product-in-use with soft blue light accents.
- Avoid busy backgrounds; favor clean surfaces.

## Tone and voice
- Short sentences, clear benefit statements.
- Avoid jargon; focus on outcomes.

## Example usage
- Primary CTA: Signal Blue background, white text.
- Secondary CTA: White background, Signal Blue outline.
